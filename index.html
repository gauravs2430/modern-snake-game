Modern Snake

A clean browser take on the original grid-based Snake game.

Score 0
State Running

Use arrow keys or WASD to move.

Paused

Press Space or Resume to continue.

Controls

Arrow keys or WASD on keyboard, buttons on touch screens.

Space pauses. R or Enter restarts after the round ends.

How to play

  • Guide the snake toward the food.
  • Each food pickup grows the snake by one segment.
  • Avoid walls and your own body.
  • Pause any time, then restart for a fresh run.
